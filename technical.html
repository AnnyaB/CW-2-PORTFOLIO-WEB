Swarm Intelligence

Swarm Intelligence refers to the collective behavior of decentralized, self-organized systems. Think of it like how a group of birds or ants work together to achieve complex tasks, without any one individual being in charge. In technology, this concept is applied to make robots or drones work together as a team, especially in tasks like space exploration or environmental monitoring.

I see swarm intelligence as the key to unlocking the potential of coordinated robot and drone fleets in space. These systems can work together to explore, analyze, and uncover the universe's deepest secrets, just like a well-coordinated team working toward a common goal.
